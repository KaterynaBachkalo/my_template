How to Play

Blast Like a Pro in 3 Easy Steps

  • 1. Tap cubes of the same color to pop them
  • 2. Match 5+ cubes to create boosters like rockets & disco blasts
  • 3. Clear goals with limited moves to unlock sweet prizes!

Keep an eye on tricky barriers like candy walls, jelly traps, and surprise boxes — and don’t forget to feed the kittens!

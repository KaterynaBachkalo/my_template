About the Game

Not Just a Puzzle — It’s a Purr-fect Adventure

Kitten Cube Blast is a vibrant, quirky, and totally adorable blast game. Match 2 or more cubes, pop your way through candy chaos, and solve unique puzzles packed with charm. Every level is cuter, crazier, and more colorful than the last.

  • Relaxing gameplay
  • Puzzle-driven storytelling
  • Tactile satisfaction with every pop
